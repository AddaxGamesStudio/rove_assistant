It’s Not Over
The Hastadilling is strong, even in your mind. But you’ve done this before. You do your foul surgery once again, and dismantle the corrupted monstrosity. 
****You think me but one thing Rovers? After all we’ve done! I am everything! I am the water in the well, I am the dark of the shadow - it is all mine for it is all me!****
The psychogeography rends asunder - a shadow twists and wretches itself out from the shadow of a stone into a giant simulacrum of a Crown, the grass grows a bloated, viscous body of muscle akin to a Terranape, the sky congeals into armored body of a mutated Gruv. All of your mind’s world attacks.
