While I Weep
Clattering wood might be a cause for concern were you in less of a hurry. Casting chunks of the dilapidated structure aside, a shockingly pristine artifact is revealed. A wooden sculpture of a Yanshif in majestic, full shift with nary a scratch along its surface. The figure depicted stands with regal bearing, its wings smooth and wrapped snug against its body. Their beak, a long and smooth implement for calling out into the faceless dark. Three wide, gleaming eyes allow for perfect vision in the cover of night. A severe looking crest of smaller feathers emanates from their brow, giving off the appearance of horns.
It churns with a controlled flow of Morph ether. Not the source of the contamination, though a possible tool for controlling it as Mo briefly considered earlier in your foray here.
