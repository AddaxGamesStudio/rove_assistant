Arboretum
*Entry within The Barren Quire from “Mockhorn,” Scribe of The Wake.*
*That we see azoth so frequently these days strikes me as something of a portent. The landscape must be changing. Perhaps the etheric balance is so heavily weighted towards the Major Anima that they can thrive in what should be a hostile environment. If only we had the resources to study the conditions here without the constant threat of death…*
Your rush to ascend the chambers of the Bonespire is halted by a realm of twisted verdure that mocks the natural order with otherworldly geometry. A massive, writhing azoth twists and undulates in the center of the chamber. Each swing of its boughs challenges your senses.
Time to cut your way through.
