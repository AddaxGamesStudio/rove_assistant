Severed Connection
The starling cores along Andiron’s left flank dim as you successfully begin to poison the lodestone. With a choked yelp the beast crashes down as its legs give out. Eclipse gracefully sails over Andiron’s twin heads and lands in a crouch. He glances back at Andiron’s twitching form as his face slowly contorts into a snarl. 
“**Do not think yourselves accomplished just yet. Andiron is but one tool in my arsenal,**” he spreads out his arms as ether starts streaming into every one of his stolen cores. “**I have others,**” he continues as the Morph begins to separate from him and streak towards Andiron, “**and I shall soon have Andiron by my side once more, after I have crafted him anew.**”
