Favors Court Thy Smile
Makaal waves excitedly from his hidey-hole. “Any of the smaller containers are yours to keep!  Part of this chaos is my trying to carry too much between visits to clan settlements, so I think we both benefit from you all trying to lighten the load!” The curled up arkenyn chuffs loudly following the merchant’s proclamation.
Fortunately, most of the smaller boxes scattered about contain stores of lyst; the favored form of currency across Lalos. Ether comes in many forms throughout the world but one of the most common is crystallized chunks of rock which vary wildly in size. Lyst is one of the smallest sizes of crystallized ether, often used in a variety of crafting disciplines both practical and cosmetic, and in some cases considered a valuable energizing vittle for starlings in particular.
