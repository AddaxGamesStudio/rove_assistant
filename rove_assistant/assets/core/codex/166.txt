Collected An Echo of the Past
“I sense there’s something more to these imbued shards that just their ether, considerable though that sum may be,” Silky comments, his aerios peering at the recovered artifact. “There is,” Mo responds with a nod. “ They were formed using a similar, though modified, process to that which we use in building our archives. As such they contain not only infused ether—” 
“—they also contain the knowledge of how to wield said ether?” Silky cuts in. Mo nods, contemplating the shard. It will work, they are now sure of it. Two more shards and they can make it happen. It only makes sense, after all, Eclipse is using starling techniques as the source of his power, so it is by starling techniques he can be defeated.
