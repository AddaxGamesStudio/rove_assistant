Whose Art Sublimely Shines
Mo detests the little therans. Haunts would be an uncommon sight for most throughout Lalos, but their travels with Makaal put them in the path of the greedy pests with surprising frequency. Nu’s core was always an attractive target for a haunt’s pilfering endeavors—it was only the additional wares kept with them that served as a better distraction.
Encountering a haunt in the Unsouled Barrens struck Mo as strange. Not only were they skilled at escaping detection, but their favorite locales were almost always the empty homes of forgotten clans. Had the Barrens stretched so far so quickly?
