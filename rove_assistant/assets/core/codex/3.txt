Some Little Cheering
“One of the most potent weapons of the galeaper is their massive and piercing tarsal claws. Easily able to shear through wood, to say nothing of flesh, whereafter their sharp mandibles can consume the innards of their given target. Silvan life has developed a number of different defensive features to deal with the constant threat of the proverbial blade and lance of the entoman that would feast upon them. 
“People, too, have developed myriad means to contend with predators. Don’t let their numbers overwhelm you. You are not a rooted plant; you are a highly intelligent being. Put distance between yourself and the enemy as needed, minimize their advantages by utilizing your own, and consider how you might deal with the habits that your enemies exhibit,” chimes Silky.
