A Tighter Clasp
Nothing to be found in this unsorted refuse… probably. Focusing on the task is made significantly more difficult by the wailing of the broken vessels fluttering throughout the settlement. Occasionally they can even be seen colliding and attempting to wrangle with each other, but whatever they hope to achieve is fruitlessly abandoned after some time and they float away in opposite directions.
Mo’s only “advice” for the Rovers is a request to make their demise swift.
