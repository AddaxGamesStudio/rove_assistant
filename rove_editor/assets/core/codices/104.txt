In Her Tomb
Despite the furious inhabitants of this grove being defeated, the forest itself has hardly calmed. The wind, the branches, the ground itself still feels as though it writhes in agony. Distant screeching echoes through the trees, surrounding you in a growing atmosphere of disturbing emotions.
Mo steps forward uneasily and lashes out a few tendrils into the air. “Beyond the trees, something… stirs? The sense is vague but tangible. It almost speaks to me. I can’t say that I am certain, but it’s as if life is struggling against itself. They’re… forlorn.”
